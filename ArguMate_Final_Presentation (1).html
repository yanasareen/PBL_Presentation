ARGUMATE

"An all-in-one debate management platform designed to make debates more organized, fair, and easier to conduct through automated topic generation, team assignment, timing, scoring, and results."

Problem Statement

Organizing debates and MUNs manually is difficult and time-consuming. There is no single digital system that helps with topic generation, team creation, scoring, and feedback all in one place. This causes confusion, errors, and wasted time for both students and organizers.

Literature Review / Market Research

Existing platforms like DebateArt, MUNBase, Tabroom focus on either online debates or MUN logistics separately. None provide integrated topic generation, team assignment, timer, scoring, and leaderboards in one platform. Most event tasks are handled manually causing delays and errors.

Research Gap / Innovation

ArguMate is the first unified platform supporting both Debate and MUN events with complete automation of event flow, transparent digital scoring, and instant results generation - designed specifically for educational institutions to reduce 70-80% manual workload.

System Methodology

Topic Generator

AI-assisted topic generation based on categories: Politics, Technology, Environment, Social Issues. Automatic selection and distribution to teams.

Team Assignment & Workflow

Automated fair division into "For" and "Against" teams. Built-in speech timer and digital scoring system with predefined evaluation criteria (Content, Delivery, Rebuttal, Clarity).

Results & Analysis

Efficiency Improvement 85%

ArguMate reduces manual workload by 70-80%, ensures fair judgment through standardized digital scoring, eliminates result delays with instant leaderboard generation, and provides complete digital record-keeping for debates and MUNs.
